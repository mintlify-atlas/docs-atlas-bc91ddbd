The Item Tracking page provides a comprehensive database of all items in Escape from Tarkov, with filtering, pricing data, tier rankings, and detailed information about how items are used in quests, barters, and crafts.

## Item database

The Items page displays all in-game items with:

**Item icon**
- Official game icon
- Visual identification

**Item name**
- Full item name
- Type categorization

**Pricing information**
- Current flea market price
- Trader buy/sell prices
- Price per slot (value density)

**Tier display**
- S/A/B/C/D tier ranking
- Indicates item value and importance

## Filtering items

The filter bar at the top helps you narrow down the item list:

### Search

Type in the search box to filter items by name. Results update in real-time as you type.

### Item type filter

Select one or more item types:
- Ammo
- Armor
- Backpack
- Barter item
- Container
- Food/Drink
- Glasses
- Grenade
- Headwear
- Key
- Magazine
- Medical
- Mod (weapon attachments)
- Rig (tactical vests)
- Weapon
- And more

<Tip>
Combine type filters to find specific item categories, like "Armor + Rig" to see all protective gear.
</Tip>

### Tier filter

Filter by tier ranking:
- **S tier** - Highest value, most important items
- **A tier** - High value items
- **B tier** - Medium value items
- **C tier** - Low value items
- **D tier** - Minimal value items

Select multiple tiers to see items across those rankings.

### Task relation filter

Filter items by their quest usage:

**Used in tasks**
- Items required as objectives or turn-ins for quests
- Example: Salewas for Therapist quests

**Received from tasks**
- Items given as quest rewards
- Example: Keys or weapons from quest completion

**Kappa required**
- Items needed for Kappa-required quests
- Prioritized for progression tracking

**Collector quest**
- Items specifically needed for the "Collector" quest
- The final Kappa requirement

<Note>
The task relation filters help you prioritize which items to keep vs. sell based on your quest progression.
</Note>

### Sort options

Sort the item list by:
- **Name** (A-Z or Z-A)
- **Price** (high to low or low to high)
- **Tier** (S to D or D to S)
- **Type** (alphabetical)

Click the sort header or use the sort dropdown to change the order.

## Tier system

Items are ranked using a tier system from S (best) to D (worst). Tiers are determined by:

- Flea market price
- Value per slot
- Quest usage frequency
- Barter/craft importance
- Rarity and availability

### Default tier thresholds

The app includes default tier thresholds based on item value and usage:

- **S tier:** 500,000₽+ or critical quest items
- **A tier:** 100,000₽ - 500,000₽
- **B tier:** 25,000₽ - 100,000₽
- **C tier:** 5,000₽ - 25,000₽
- **D tier:** Under 5,000₽

<Tip>
Tier rankings help you quickly identify which items are worth keeping, which to sell, and which to prioritize for quests.
</Tip>

## Customizable tier thresholds

You can customize the tier boundaries to match your personal preferences or economy state.

### Accessing tier settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the settings icon">
    On the Items page, click the gear icon in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Tier Settings modal">
    A modal appears with tier threshold controls.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust thresholds">
    Modify the price boundaries for each tier.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click Save to apply your custom thresholds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Custom threshold examples

**Early wipe settings:**
- Lower thresholds since prices are higher
- More items fall into S/A tiers

**Late wipe settings:**
- Raise thresholds as the economy inflates
- Adjust for devalued items

**Personal value preferences:**
- Prioritize items you need for your current quests
- Downrank items you don't care about

<Note>
Custom tier thresholds are saved locally and persist across sessions. You can reset to defaults at any time.
</Note>

## Item detail view

Click any item to open the detail modal with comprehensive information:

### Basic information

- **Item name**
- **Type/category**
- **Base stats** (armor class, durability, caliber, etc.)
- **Size** (grid slots)
- **Weight**

### Pricing data

**Flea market**
- Current lowest offer price
- Price per slot
- 24-hour price history (if available)

**Trader prices**
- Buy prices from each trader
- Sell prices to each trader
- Best buy/sell options highlighted

### Task usage

**Used in tasks (Find in Raid)**
- List of quests requiring this item
- Quantities needed
- Trader who assigns the quest
- Kappa requirement indicator

**Received from tasks**
- Quests that reward this item
- Quantities received
- Whether it's a guaranteed or conditional reward

<Tip>
The "Used in tasks" section helps you decide whether to hoard an item for upcoming quests or sell it immediately.
</Tip>

### Barter information

**Barter trades**
- All trader barters that accept this item as payment
- What you receive in exchange
- Trader and loyalty level required

**Crafts**
- All hideout crafts that use this item as an ingredient
- Output items
- Which station and level required

**Barters for this item**
- Trades where you can obtain this item
- What items you need to trade
- More cost-effective than flea market in some cases

### Other uses

- **Keys:** Which doors/rooms they open
- **Ammo:** Penetration, damage, and ballistics
- **Armor:** Protection zones and class
- **Mods:** Compatible weapons and stats

## View modes

The Items page supports two view modes:

### Tier view (default)

Groups items by their tier ranking:

- Sections for S, A, B, C, and D tiers
- Collapsible sections
- Count of items in each tier
- Easy comparison within tiers

**Use Tier view when:**
- You want to browse high-value items
- You're evaluating what to keep vs. sell
- You need to see relative item importance

### Grid view

Displays all items in a flat grid:

- Uniform card layout
- Sortable by any column
- No tier grouping
- Faster scanning of many items

**Use Grid view when:**
- You're searching for a specific item
- You want to sort by price or name
- You prefer a more traditional list layout

Switch between views using the toggle buttons at the top of the item list.

## User workflows

### Finding quest items

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply the task relation filter">
    Select "Used in tasks" or "Kappa required".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the filtered list">
    See all items needed for quests.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click an item for details">
    Check which specific quests need it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sort by tier or price">
    Prioritize high-value quest items.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark tasks that need these items">
    Cross-reference with the Tasks page to plan collection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Evaluating loot value

<Steps>
  <Step title="Switch to Tier view">
    Browse items grouped by value tier.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Expand the S and A tier sections">
    Focus on the highest-value items.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Note price per slot">
    Identify the most valuable items for limited inventory space.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check barter and craft uses">
    See if items are worth more in trades than on the flea.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a mental priority list">
    Remember which items to always pick up in-raid.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Planning barter trades

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search for the item you want">
    Find the item you'd like to obtain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the item details">
    Click the item to view full information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review barter trades">
    Check the "Barters for this item" section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compare costs">
    Calculate if the barter is cheaper than flea market.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Note required items">
    Cross-reference barter requirements with your stash.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Preparing for Collector quest

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply the Collector filter">
    Show only items needed for the Collector quest.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sort by price">
    Identify the most expensive items to collect.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check task usage">
    See if any items overlap with other quests.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Find in Raid requirements">
    Determine which items must be found in-raid vs. purchased.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a shopping list">
    Plan which items to buy and which to find.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Customizing tiers for your economy

<Steps>
  <Step title="Monitor current flea prices">
    Check if tier rankings match current market values.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open tier settings">
    Click the settings icon on the Items page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust threshold values">
    Modify tier boundaries based on inflation or deflation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and review">
    Check if the new tiers match your expectations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Iterate as needed">
    Refine thresholds throughout the wipe as the economy changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>
